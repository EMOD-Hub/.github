# The EMOD Community

The EMOD community includes researchers, software developers, and collaborators
working in infectious disease modeling, including malaria and HIV research.

We value openness, mutual respect, collaboration, and constructive discussion.

To ask questions, share ideas, discuss workflows, or connect with other users and
contributors, please visit the EMOD-Hub GitHub Discussions space:

https://github.com/orgs/EMOD-Hub/discussions
